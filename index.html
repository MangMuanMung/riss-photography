Download Your Photos & Videos

Welcome! Your photos and videos have been shared via Google Drive. Follow these steps to download your memories to your device.

01

Open the Link

Check your email or message for the Google Drive link. Click to open in your browser. No Google account required.

02

View Your Files

Browse through your photos and videos. Preview them to see all your captured moments.

03

Select Files

Choose what to download:

  • Single file: Click directly on it
  • All files: Select the folder name
  • Multiple files: Check boxes next to items

04

Download

Click the download button (top right) or use the three-dot menu. For multiple files, right-click and select download.

05

Processing

Download starts automatically. Multiple files compress to ZIP. Large videos may take a few minutes based on connection speed.

06

Access Files

Find files in your Downloads folder. ZIP files need extraction: right-click "Extract All" (Windows) or double-click (Mac).

Pro Tips

Mobile Users

Use the Google Drive app for easier access. Tap three dots, select download.

Storage Space

Ensure sufficient device space before downloading, especially for videos.

Connection

Use WiFi for large downloads to preserve mobile data.
